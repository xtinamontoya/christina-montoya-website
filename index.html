About

Restaurant and bar leadership with backbone and warmth.

Christina Montoya brings the practical discipline of an operator, the instincts of a host, and the calm confidence required when the dining room is full, the bar is three-deep, and everyone still expects magic.

Her background spans restaurant and bar leadership, team development, service standards, guest recovery, shift execution, inventory, scheduling, cocktail curation, and building the kind of culture guests feel the moment they walk into the restaurant or take a seat at the bar.

The experience is the product.

Great restaurant and bar hospitality is more than food and drink. It is timing, tone, confidence, warmth, pace, and control. The guest may not notice every detail, but they feel all of it.

Hospitality philosophy

Make service feel effortless, even when it is not.

The best restaurant and bar service has rhythm. Guests feel seen, teams feel supported, and service moves with confidence. Behind that feeling is structure: clear standards, thoughtful training, precise communication, and leaders who know when to step in without taking over.

That balance is where Christina does her best work.
